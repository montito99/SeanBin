Q: What is SeanBin?

SeanBin is the cryptographic PasteBin for your photos.

Q: What encrypting algorithms does it use?

SeanBin utilizes SJCL, the Stanford JScript Cryptography Library. Read more about it here

Q: How does SeanBin secure your photos?

By encrypting your photo on the client side, and sending only the cipher to our servers.

Q: How did SeanBin get its name?

SeanBin is a pun on the name PasteBin and the actor Sean Bean.
